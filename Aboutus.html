ABOUT HOSHI STUDIO

Hoshi Studio was born from a passion for anime and the desire to bring fans closer to the characters and stories they love.

We carefully curate high-quality figures and merchandise, ensuring every product we offer meets the standards of true collectors.

Founded in 2020, we started as a small group of dedicated anime enthusiasts who wanted to create a one-stop destination for authentic, premium collectibles. What began as a passion project has grown into a trusted source for fans worldwide.

Our team travels across Japan to discover the latest releases, limited editions, and hidden gems that will delight collectors of all levels. We work directly with licensed manufacturers to guarantee authenticity and quality in every item we sell.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. Let's continue to celebrate anime together!
